Administrator Help Page

Administrator Manual

First login with your admin user name and password.

Then, all the public images, your images and images from all your group will be displayed. By clicking the thumbnail image, you will be directed to the original image with all the information of the image. If you are the owner of the image, you can modify all the details and security specification of the image.

If you wish to upload images to your own album, click the upload image button; it will lead you to the upload page. Click Choose File, then choose your image and hit the Upload button. After that, entered all the details and security specification and hit update. By default, all the images you uploaded are private.

If you wish to edit your groups, click to Edit Group button. You can create a new group, delete a group; or you can edit your group member.

If you wish to search certain images, click the Search button. Enter key words or time period to do the search, choose the way to sort the data, either by score or timing, i.e., most recent first or most recent last. The thumbnail image and its details will be displayed.

If you wish to do some data analysis, hit the admin button. Enter all the keywords you want to either user, subject or both; and/or the time period you would like to see, then choose whether you would to like to see by year, month or week.

Lastly, hit the log out button to log off.
