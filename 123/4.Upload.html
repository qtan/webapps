4.1 Upload Image

To upload an image, click the “Upload Image” button from the profile page.

From this page, choose the file you would like to upload (in the format of jpeg, jpg or gif) by clicking the browse button.

  • You can also enter descriptive information about the image right after you submit the pictures.
  • The information including subject, place, permission, the date you took the image and description are all editable.
  • As was previously mention in the security module. We can then select the permission desired for said image.
  • When uploading a batch of images, click the browse button, and hold the the CTRL key to select multiple files.
  • When uploading in this batch method; it's descriptive information is also editable as a batch.
  • *If you choose not to enter descriptive information, the security specification will default to public, and the rest of the information will remain as default null value.
