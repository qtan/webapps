2.1 Register an Account

  • Before you start your image management, your must first register an account. You should provide a:
  • username
  • password
  • first name
  • last name
  • email address
  • mailing address
  • phone number
  • If your signup failed, do not worry, you can easily reset it by pressing reset button, or wait 5 seconds to refresh the page.
  • If your signup succeeded you will be redirected back to the login page automatically or you can click on the return button provided


    • *For security reasons the username and email address you provide should be unique

2.2 Login

  • After being redirected back to the welcome page you simply login by

  • Entering your registered username in the first text box

  • Then entering your registered password in the second

  • If you made mistake entering your username and/or password, you can press the reset button or wait for five seconds to refresh the welcome page.
